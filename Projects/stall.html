Stalls

All members of our collective can contribute when organizing a stall.
Since we are a collective of zine makers, screenprinters, tattooers and general art print makers
we have lots of affordable goodies to sell, we are open for trading and donations.
We apply multiple times a year for stalls in fairs that we like :)
If you can't manage to come to the stalls, you can buy our some of our things in our Etsy shop
