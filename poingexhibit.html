Poing exhibition

We organise exhibitions together :)
We recently organised one in a night club in Rotterdam:

On 23-3-2023,
UFF's extensive archive will come to life in the basement of Poing.
With an automatic video-cutting system that randomly selects raw footage,
renderings, and animations from our archive,
you can experience snippets of our work. On the big screen,
you can enjoy an installation where shapes morph to the sounds of carefully curated ambient techno.
Similar to an anti-stress ball morphing into different shapes.
